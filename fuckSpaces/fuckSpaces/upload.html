What is Happening?

E-Waste in Singapore

Do you know how much E-Waste is being generated in Singapore? A staggering 60,000 tonnes of E-Waste is being generated annually in Singapore. To put this into perspective, that number adds up to about 70 mobile phones per person! Imagine that!


Why is E-Waste dangerous?

These discarded electronic products contains batteries or plugs which contains toxic and hazardous contaminants that pose a severe risk to both human and environmental health. These risks include cancer, neurological damage and climate change.

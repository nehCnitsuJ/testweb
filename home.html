Welcome To aikiForest

Aikido is a Japanese self-defense martial arts which is often translated as "the way of harmonious spirit". The primary goal of aikido is to overcome oneself instead of cultivating violence and aggressivess, said by the founder of Aikido - Ueshiba Morihei. This involves throwing and twisting techniques which aims in turning the attacker's momentum against himself. At aikiForest, we offer various classes ranging from children to youth & adult. Students will be able to learn movement & technique fundamentals from basic to advance levels based on their age groups.
